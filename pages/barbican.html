MAP Curious is a free annual showcase of new works created by students promoted by Barbican and Guildhall Creative Learning. The project is in collaboration with graduates and professional artists from the Guildhall School of Music and Drama and designers from the Graphic Communication Design programme at Central Saint Martins.

The exhibition was set up to be as one immersive experience, and the audience was guided through the space from one piece to the other connecting the whole together.

My personal piece, as seen below, was in collaboration with Hannah Fredsgaard-Jones and Mahmoud Said Ahmed. Together we decided to work on the concept of greenhouse and greenhouse gases as a metaphore for talking about the planet and its delicate balance.
