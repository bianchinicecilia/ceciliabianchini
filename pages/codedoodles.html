Codedoodl.es is an archive of curated creative coding sketches created by FLUUUID and sponsored by Nexus Interactive Arts.
For this identit y I’ve decided to play on the concept of framing and sketches.
The logo is contained in a rectangular frame representing the browser window.
This is labeled as “.es”.

In the small version of the logo the shape is declined, becoming the arena where to play with the doodles, symbolized here by the red sketches.

The font Space Mono has been selected because, thanks to its fixed width, it’s a widely used typeface in coding related artworks.
