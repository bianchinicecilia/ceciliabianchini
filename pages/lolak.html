Lola K, where K stands for “pearl”, is an Egyptian fashion brand producing luxury footwear.

The peculiarity of the shoes lies in their heels, made interchangeable thanks to a bespoken mechanism enabling their removal. The initial collection compris es 5 silhouette and three heels, each of which stems from the collaboration with an artist - making every pair of Lola K one of a kind.

While celebrating middle east cultural heritage, the brand wants to encourage Arabic women to push their own boundaries, and accommodate their pers onal lifestyle.
